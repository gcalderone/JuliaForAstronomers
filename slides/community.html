Julia community

Small but very active: computer scientists, engineers, scientists, persons involved in all kinds of numerical computing

(May 2018)

  • Main website, http://julialang.org: best starting point;
  • The discourse, https://discourse.julialang.org: with $\sim$3200 registered and active users, the best place to search for detailed informations and ask questions;
  • Github, https://github.com/JuliaLang/julia: core language development, with 687 contributors;
  • Contributed package repository, http://pkg.julialang.org: 1827 registered packages;
  • JuliaCon, http://juliacon.org/: annual Julia conference, this year in London;
  • Youtube channel, https://www.youtube.com/user/JuliaLanguage: 6700 subscribers, and all JuliaCon videos available;
