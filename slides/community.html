Julia community (May 2018)

Small but very active: computer scientists, engineers, scientists, many enthusiastic persons involved in all kinds of numerical computing

  • Main website, http://julialang.org: best starting point;
  • The discourse, https://discourse.julialang.org: with $\sim$12,400 topics, $\sim$100,000 posts, $\sim$5,400 registered and active users, the best place to search for detailed informations and ask questions;
  • Github, https://github.com/JuliaLang/julia: core language development, with 795 contributors;
  • Contributed package repository, https://pkg.julialang.org/docs/: $\sim$2,200 registered packages;
  • JuliaCon, http://juliacon.org/: annual Julia conference;
  • Youtube channel, https://www.youtube.com/user/JuliaLanguage: 12,500 subscribers, and all JuliaCon videos available;
