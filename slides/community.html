Julia community (Jan 2021)

Small but very active: computer scientists, engineers, scientists, many enthusiastic persons involved in all kinds of numerical computing

  • Main website, http://julialang.org: best starting point;
  • The discourse, https://discourse.julialang.org: with $\sim$29.7k topics, $\sim$260k posts, $\sim$12k registered users, the best place to search for detailed informations and ask questions (stats);
  • Github, https://github.com/JuliaLang/julia: core language development, with $\sim$1k contributors;
  • Julia ecosystem (Contributed package repositories), https://juliahub.com/ui/Home: $\sim$5k registered packages;
  • JuliaCon, http://juliacon.org/: annual Julia conference;
  • Youtube channel, https://www.youtube.com/user/JuliaLanguage: $\sim$52k subscribers,all JuliaCon videos available;
