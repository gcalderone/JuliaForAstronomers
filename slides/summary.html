Summary

  • Julia is a new, free, high level, expressive and high-performance language aiming to solve the two-language problem;
  • Julia provides new features (parallelization, easy metaprogramming, native high performances, etc.) which will become essential features of future languages;
  • Julia is still young: some professional library may still be missing...;
  • Julia has a very active community, mostly experts in science, computer science, mathematics, statistics, etc. It is being taught in many Universities, and adopted in many companies;
  • The Astronomy community is deeply involved in numerical computation and big data challenges, but still poorly interested in Julia (and associated technologies).
