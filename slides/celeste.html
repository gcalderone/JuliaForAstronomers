Celeste: a case study

  • Celeste is a software for stars and galaxies characterization in astronomical images, entirely written in Julia;
  • 55 TB SDSS Images $\Rightarrow$ 650k cores of the Cori supercomputer@NERSC $\Rightarrow$ position, brightness, etc... of 188M sources, in $\sim$15 minutes;
  • ... achieving a peak rate of 1.5 PFLOP/s, demonstrating that Julia excels at HPC, achieving performances previously attained only by C, C++ and Fortran.
